## Negative Keywords (Filter False Positives)

### Self-Promotion Indicators (Skip These)
- "I built"
- "I created"
- "launching my"
- "feedback on my"
- "check out my"
- "just released"
- "beta testers"
- "lifetime deal"
- "discount code"

### Low-Quality Patterns
- "upvote if"
- "please share"
- "what do you think"
- "would you use"
- "validate my idea"

## Source-Specific Patterns

### Reddit: smallbusiness (High Quality)
Common valuable patterns:
- "How do you handle [task]?"
- "What do you use for [need]?"
- "Spending too much on [tool]"
- "Manual process for [workflow]"

### Reddit: SaaS (Mixed Quality)
**Good:**
- "[Price] ARR but [problem]"
- "Competitor [tool] but [gap]"
- "Sick of paying [amount] for [tool]"

**Bad (skip):**
- "I built [tool]"
- "Feedback on [my product]"
- "Just launched [my SaaS]"

### Hacker News
**Good patterns:**
- "Ask HN: How do you [task]?"
- "Ask HN: Alternative to [tool]?"
- "Ask HN: Is there a [solution]?"

**Skip:**
- "Show HN:" (product launches)
- "Tell HN:" (opinions)

### GitHub
**High signal:**
- 5+ reactions on feature request
- Multiple comments agreeing
- "Would love to see"
- "Missing feature"

## Phrase Combinations (Extra Weight)

### Pain + Money (Strongest Signal)
- "expensive" + "frustrated"
- "pricing" + "sick of"
- "$[amount]" + "too much"

**Examples:**
- "Sick of paying $150/month for DocuSign"
- "Frustrated with expensive CRM pricing"

### Pain + Time (Productivity Opportunity)
- "manual" + "hours"
- "repetitive" + "wasting time"
- "every day" + "tedious"

**Examples:**
- "Spending hours on manual data entry every day"
- "Repetitive task wasting 10 hours/week"

### Business + Scale (B2B SaaS)
- "clients" + "growing"
- "team" + "scaling"
- "revenue" + "users"

**Examples:**
- "Growing from 10 to 50 clients, current tool doesn't scale"
- "Team is scaling but tools are breaking"
